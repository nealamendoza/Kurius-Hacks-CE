What is From Me To You?

From me to you is a website utilized to help locate food drives that are happening and to add your own food drive event. With this tool, you are able to organize an event to help donate food for those in need. By sharing where and when the event will take place, others will be able to attend the event and donate to your organization. This initiative was created to help fight hunger and food security for those who need it.

What are Food Drives?

Food drives are conducted by local organizations to help food banks, soup kitchens, and other charities to donate supplies, such as canned goods and other non-perishable items to those who cannot afford it. Local food banks rely on donations from the public to help support their initiative so holding food drives are a great way to support them are raise awareness of the situation.

What are Food Banks?

Food banks are non-profit organizations that stores the donations that was given from a food drive. These donations are distributed to food pantries or other organizations that help with serving others.
